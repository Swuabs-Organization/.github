# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Swuabs Organization is committed to providing a welcoming, professional, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** - Treat everyone with respect and consideration
- **Be professional** - Maintain professionalism in all interactions
- **Be collaborative** - Work together constructively
- **Be constructive** - Provide helpful, actionable feedback
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome and support people of all backgrounds
- **Be patient** - Remember that everyone is learning and growing
- **Be accountable** - Take responsibility for your words and actions

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Project-Specific Standards

### Code Reviews

- Focus on the code, not the person
- Provide specific, actionable suggestions
- Accept feedback graciously
- Assume good intentions

### Issues and Discussions

- Stay on topic
- Be clear and concise
- Search before posting duplicates
- Use appropriate labels and templates

### Communication

- Use appropriate channels (GitHub Issues for bugs, Discussions for questions, Discord for casual chat)
- Keep conversations professional and respectful
- Avoid spam or excessive self-promotion
- Respect others' time and attention

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to:
- **Email:** swuabsoptimizations@gmail.com
- **Contact:** Organization leadership (Founder, CHRO, or CLO)

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

### Response Process

1. **Report received** - We'll acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. **Investigation** - We'll review the situation and gather information
3. **Decision** - Leadership will determine appropriate action
4. **Follow-up** - Reporter will be informed of the outcome

### Consequences

Violations may result in:

- **Warning** - Private warning with explanation
- **Temporary Ban** - Temporary restriction from organization activities
- **Permanent Ban** - Permanent removal from the organization

The severity of consequences depends on:
- Nature and severity of the violation
- Impact on team members
- Whether it's a first offense or repeated behavior
- Response and accountability from the individual

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All organization repositories
- GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, and Discussions
- Organization Discord server
- Any other official communication channels
- Public representation of the organization
